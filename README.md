# TaTa-Gen-AI-Powered Credit Delinquency Risk Analysis

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Tata GenAI Powered Data Analytics Job Simulation** on Forage.

The objective is to analyze customer financial data, identify factors contributing to credit delinquency, build a predictive model to estimate delinquency risk, and propose an AI-driven collections strategy that improves operational efficiency while maintaining fairness and transparency.

---

## Business Problem

Financial institutions face significant losses due to customer loan delinquency. Traditional collections processes often rely on manual intervention and reactive decision-making, leading to higher operational costs and inconsistent customer experiences.

The goal of this project is to:

- Identify customers at risk of becoming delinquent.
- Develop a predictive model for early risk detection.
- Recommend an AI-powered collections strategy.
- Ensure responsible and ethical use of AI in financial decision-making.

---

## Key Insights

The analysis identified several important delinquency risk factors:

- Higher credit utilization is strongly associated with increased delinquency risk.
- Customers with multiple missed payments are significantly more likely to default.
- Debt-to-income ratio is an important financial stress indicator.
- Some high-income customers also exhibited low credit scores, requiring further investigation.
